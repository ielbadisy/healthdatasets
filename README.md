# healthdatasets

This repo contains some datasets related to biomedical sciences for educational aim. 


-------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Heart attacks and arthritis

**Name** `heart_arthritis` & `heart_arthritis2`

**Source** : data was extracted directly from the CRC website (2019)

**Variables**

 * `id` = patient's id
 * `arthritis.has` = has arthritis or not (Yes/No) ?
 * `heart.attack` = the patient had a heart attack (Yes/No) ?
 * `heart.attack.relative` = a relative of the patient has had a heart attack (Yes/No) ?
 * `gender` = sex of the patient (Female/Male)
 * `age` = age of the patient in years	
 * `bmi` = body mass index indicator
 * `diabetes` = does the patient have diabetes (Yes/No) ?
 * `alcohol` = does the patient consume alcohol (Yes/No) ?
 * `smoke` = does the patient smoke cigarettes (Yes/No) ?
 * `prehypertension` = does the patient had prehypertension (Yes/No) ?
 * `vegetarian` = is the patient vegetarian (Yes/No) ?
 * `covered.health` = does the patient had a health assurance (Yes/No) ?
